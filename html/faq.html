FAQs

Do we have a seasonal menu?

Yes, we do, but those menus are not on our webpage. Instead, they are provided once you arrives at one of our restaurants, since they changes with every season, and non-repeating. Our most famous seasonal menus are the Spring menus.

Is the owner single and ready to mingle?

Yes, he is. You can ask for Mr. Kendrick contact and social info at the cashier booth.

Do we offer party services?

No, not in general, for we aim to provide our customers with the best fine dining experience available. But, we do offer them if you purchase our party package. For more information, please contact us via our form.
