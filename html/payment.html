Pay with online banking

Step 1: Go to the Vietcombank website: https://www.vietcombank.com.vn/
Step 2: Log in → Payment → Bill payment
Step 3: Select service → Select service provider
Step 4: Select fee type from the drop-down list → Fill in the payment amount → Confirm
Step 5: Enter verification code → Confirm
Step 6: Enter OTP (Sent via SMS)
Step 7: Confirm
Step 8: Show the confirmation message to our staff

Pay with credit card

Payment using Visa or Master card is accepted at our establishment.
Simply bring the card to the cashier desk and swipe it through our POS terminal.

Pay with cash

We accept payments with cash. You can pay for your bill at our cashier counter.
